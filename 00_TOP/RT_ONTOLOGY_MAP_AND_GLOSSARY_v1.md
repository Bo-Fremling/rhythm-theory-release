### Map (one picture in words)
- **PP (Primal Plane)** is the primary arena: an **infinite xy-plane** with **time as the z-axis** (z=time, not depth). Core objects live in PP, and write traces as curves.
- **RP_strob** is a measurement view: a discrete sampling of PP at the C30 ticks (addresses, audits).
- **Overlay** is where SI enters (kappa, Hz, fm, CODATA) for comparison only.

Flow:
PP (core objects + dynamics) → RP_strob (tick-sampled addresses + audits) → Overlay (SI mapping + data comparison).

### Core objects
- **TP**: a TimeParticle (tick-driven actor) whose **trace** is a 1D curve (“cord”) in PP.
- **Phase tension / phase pressure / phase pull**: same thing in this project. A stored disharmony that can act as a source of interaction.
- **TickPulse**: the discrete, **globally synchronous** update step in PP (one universe-step per tick). “Pulse from behind” means the update is time-directed (depends on prior ticks), not that the schedule is asynchronous across PP.

### Views
- **PP**: where the theory lives. No SI constants are allowed as inputs.
- **RP_strob**: the audit/measurement sampling of PP on the discrete tick lattice.
- **Overlay**: the SI-facing layer. Kappa is chosen a posteriori for reporting/fit; it must not feed back into Core.

### Gates (locked discoveries; not yet derived from minimal postulates)
- **C30 / K=30**: the 30-slot stroboscopic gate.
- **Z2 (A/B)**: a two-branch channel used in addresses and parity/polarisation bookkeeping.
- **Z3**: a three-sector ledger on the C30 lattice. Sector label is s(k)=k mod 3. A phase offset ±2π/3 is exactly ±10 C30 steps, so Z3 offsets are representable without approximation.
  (Details: `00_TOP/RT_Z3_Z6_RHO_LEMMAS_v1.md`.)
- **No-overlap (address exclusion)**: prevents forbidden co-occupations in RP_strob for atomic ordering.
- **rho=10 (“ten-per-tick”)**: audit constraint in the rotor/helix structure.

### Forces and labels (how terms are used)
- **Phase-tension / compression field (Core)**: phase tension sources an effective potential; the inverse-square behaviour emerges in RP readout. ("Gravity" is the Overlay name for this effect.)
- **Relativity (Core)**: interpreted as projection/budget; the Lorentz factor is a RP effect of a fixed PP speed budget.
- **Mass/inertia (Core)**: a derived label: minimal locked cost (rest) plus response against change (second variation), not an extra substance.

### Notation hygiene
- **Gamma (Γ)**: Lorentz factor / projection effect.
- **gamma (γ)**: a coupling constant in older notes; never mix them.
